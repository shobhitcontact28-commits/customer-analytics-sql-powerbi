# Dataset

This folder contains the dataset used for the customer analytics project.

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains approximately 9,000 customer records from an e-commerce platform.

The data includes information about:

- Customer demographics
- Purchase behavior
- Product preferences
- Marketing engagement metrics
- Customer satisfaction
- Customer lifetime value

## Key Fields

CustomerID – Unique customer identifier  
RegistrationDate – Date when the customer registered  
Age – Customer age  
Gender – Customer gender  
IncomeLevel – Income segment of the customer  
Country – Customer location  
City – Customer city  

TotalPurchases – Total number of purchases  
AverageOrderValue – Average order value per purchase  
CustomerLifetimeValue – Total revenue generated by the customer  

FavoriteCategory – Most preferred product category  
SecondFavoriteCategory – Second preferred product category  

EmailEngagementRate – Email interaction rate  
SocialMediaEngagementRate – Social media engagement rate  

AverageSatisfactionScore – Customer satisfaction rating  

Conversion metrics include:

- EmailConversionRate
- SocialMediaConversionRate
- SearchEngineConversionRate

## Note

When running the SQL scripts, update the BULK INSERT file path in `01_data_import.sql` to match the location of the dataset on your local machine.
